BLT Sandwich

Toasted sourdough bread with thinly spread mayo and 2 slices of thick cut applewood smoked bacon. Heirloom tomatoes dusted with salt and pepper and crisp butter lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices - Sourdough Bread
  • 2-4 Slices Applewood Smoked Bacon
  • 2 Pieces - Butter Lettuce
  • 2 Slices - Heirloom Tomato
  • Butter
  • Mayo to taste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a skillet or cast iron pan cook 2-4 slices of bacon.
  2. Remove cooked bacon and drain oil from pan.
  3. Butter both sides of each slice of sourdough bread and toast in pan.
  4. Spread mayo on toasted bread.
  5. Cut 2 slices of tomato, add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Assemble sandwich in the following order: bread, lettuce, tomato, bacon, bread.
  7. Optional: Slice sandwich diagonally.
  8. Enjoy!
