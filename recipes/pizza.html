Pepperoni, Black Olive, and Jalapeno Pizza

Spicy jalapenos on a classic combo of pepperoni and black olives.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Sliced Black Olives
  • Sliced Jalapenos
  • Rosemary, salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough on greased pan.
  2. Layer Sauce, Mozzarella, and Pepperoni on dough.
  3. Finish layering with black olives and jalapenos.
  4. Sprinkle crust and pizza with salt/peppr and herbs to taste.
  5. Bake at 450 degrees for 20 minutes until crust and cheese are golden brown. (Cooking time may vary)
  6. Slice and enjoy!
