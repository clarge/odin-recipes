Breakfast Burrito

Crunchy breakfast burrito to be enjoyed at any time of day. Potato egg and cheese. Avocado and cooked steak or bacon.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Diced and cooked potato or hashbrowns
  • Eggs
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt, pepper, hot sauce
  • (Optional) Sliced avocado, cooked steak or bacon

Steps

  1. Scramble and cook eggs adding salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Add layer of shredded cheddar to tortilla.
  3. Add eggs and potatos (or hashbrowns).
  4. Optional: add avocado, cooked steak, or bacon.
  5. Wrap burrito and lightly toast on all sides.
  6. Enjoy with hot sauce!
